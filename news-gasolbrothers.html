Pau and Marc Gasol made history by becoming the first brothers in NBA history to have their jerseys retired. Their careers are celebrated for remarkable achievements both in Europe and the NBA. Pau's leadership and Marc's versatility on the court have inspired countless players, and their legacy will be remembered by fans around the world. This retirement ceremony marks a historic milestone not only for the Gasol family but also for international players making an impact in the NBA.

Throughout their careers, both brothers have exemplified professionalism and dedication. Pau's time with the Memphis Grizzlies and Los Angeles Lakers, along with Marc's stints with the Memphis Grizzlies and Toronto Raptors, have cemented their status as legends of the game. Their influence extends beyond basketball, inspiring youth programs and international basketball development.

Fans can revisit memorable moments, career highlights, and interviews from both brothers, as their impact continues to resonate. This recognition honors not only their individual contributions but also the cultural bridge they built between European basketball and the NBA.
